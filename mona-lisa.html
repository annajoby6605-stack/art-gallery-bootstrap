Mona Lisa

Artist: Leonardo da Vinci

Year: 1503 - 1506

Country: Italy

Style: Renaissance

Current Location: Louvre Museum, Paris, France

The Mona Lisa is one of the most famous paintings in the world and is widely considered a masterpiece of the Italian Renaissance. Painted by Leonardo da Vinci, the portrait is believed to depict Lisa Gherardini, the wife of a Florentine merchant. The painting is renowned for its mysterious smile, realistic facial expressions, and advanced painting techniques.
