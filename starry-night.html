The Starry Night

Artist: Vincent van Gogh

Year: 1889

Country: Netherlands

Style: Post-Impressionism

Current Location: Museum of Modern Art, New York

The Starry Night is one of Vincent van Gogh's most celebrated paintings. It was created while the artist was staying at the Saint-Paul-de-Mausole asylum in France. The painting depicts a peaceful village beneath a dramatic sky filled with swirling stars and a glowing moon.
