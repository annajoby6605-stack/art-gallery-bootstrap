Starry Night

Artist

Vincent van Gogh

Year of Creation

1889

Description

Starry Night is one of Vincent van Gogh's most famous paintings. It depicts a peaceful village beneath a swirling night sky filled with bright stars and a glowing moon. The artwork is celebrated for its bold colors, expressive brushstrokes, and emotional depth.
