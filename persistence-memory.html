The Persistence of Memory

Artist

Salvador Dalí

Year of Creation

1931

Description

The Persistence of Memory is one of Salvador Dalí's most famous surrealist paintings. It is well known for its melting clocks, which symbolize the fluid nature of time and memory. The painting encourages viewers to think beyond reality and explore imagination.
