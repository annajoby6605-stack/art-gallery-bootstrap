Artist

Year of Creation

Description

The Scream is one of the most recognizable paintings in the world. Created by Edvard Munch, it expresses feelings of fear, anxiety, and emotional intensity through dramatic colors and a striking composition. It is considered a masterpiece of Expressionist art.
