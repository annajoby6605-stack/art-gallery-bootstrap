The Scream

Artist: Edvard Munch

Year: 1893

Country: Norway

Style: Expressionism

Current Location: National Museum, Oslo, Norway

The Scream is one of the most iconic artworks in history and is often regarded as a symbol of human anxiety and fear. The painting portrays a figure standing on a bridge with a distressed expression while the sky glows with dramatic colors.
